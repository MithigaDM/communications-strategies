Key Challenges in GenAI Adoption

While GenAI shows promise, enterprises face several interrelated barriers to widespread success and profitability:

Strategic Barriers

  • Unclear Use Cases: Adoption due to trends, not defined problems; leads to pilot purgatory.
  • Data Quality Issues: Siloed, unstructured data causes hallucinations and reduced trust.
  • High Costs: Expensive compute and hidden costs; uncertain ROI for some apps.

Operational Barriers

  • Security Risks: Data leakage in APIs; lack of governance and IP uncertainties.
  • Talent Gaps: Shortage of skilled professionals; employee resistance and rigid processes.
  • Immature Tooling: Probabilistic outputs hard to measure; evolving MLOps.

Technology Barriers

  • Vendor Fragmentation: Rapid changes create integration risks; choices between models and deployments.
